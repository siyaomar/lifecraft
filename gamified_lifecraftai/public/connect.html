Frequently Asked Questions
Is LifeCraft completely free to use?
Yes. All core features — resume builder, ATS analyzer, skill gap detector, interview prep, and career roadmaps — are 100% free. We may introduce optional premium features later, but the essentials will always stay free.
How accurate is the ATS score?
Our scoring is based on keyword analysis, structural best practices, and content quality metrics aligned with how top ATS systems like Workday, Greenhouse, and Lever evaluate resumes. Resumes scoring 75+ consistently perform well in automated screening.
Is my resume data stored or sent anywhere?
No. All resume data is saved locally in your browser's localStorage — nothing is transmitted to our servers. Your information stays completely private and on your device.
Can I use LifeCraft for non-tech careers?
The current module is optimised for tech careers (web dev, data science, cloud, AI/ML, cybersecurity, UI/UX). Support for management, finance, and other domains is on our roadmap — reach out to vote for your field!
How can I contribute to LifeCraft?
We love contributors! You can open issues or pull requests on our GitHub, suggest features using the form above, or simply share LifeCraft with students who need it. Every bit of feedback directly shapes the platform.
Do you offer any mentorship or 1-on-1 career guidance?
Not yet, but it's on our roadmap! Join our Discord community in the meantime — many experienced professionals there actively help students with career questions. Drop a message to our team if you'd like to be notified when mentorship launches.
