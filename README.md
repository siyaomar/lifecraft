# 🌱 LifeCraft AI

## Transforming Personal & Professional Growth Through AI 

LifeCraft AI is an intelligent platform designed to support **holistic growth** by combining **career development, mental wellness, productivity, and sustainable lifestyle awareness** into a single guided ecosystem.

Unlike traditional platforms that focus on only one aspect of self-improvement, LifeCraft AI helps users grow professionally and personally through AI-powered recommendations, structured guidance, and self-reflection tools.

---

### 🚀 Problem Statement

Students and early professionals often face multiple challenges:

* Lack of clear career direction
* Poor resume quality
* Uncertainty about which skills to learn
* Scattered learning resources
* Unstructured interview preparation
* Growing stress and lack of self-reflection

Although many tools exist, they are fragmented and disconnected.

LifeCraft AI bridges this gap by providing a unified platform for growth.

---

# ✨ Features

## 🎯 Career Development Module

### 📄 Smart Resume Builder

Create professional and ATS-friendly resumes with:

* Real-time editing
* Live preview
* Clean professional templates
* Downloadable resume formats

### 📊 ATS Resume Analysis

Analyze resumes based on:

* Structure and formatting
* Keyword optimization
* Readability
* Content quality

Users receive an ATS score along with personalized improvement suggestions.

### 🔍 Skill Extraction & Gap Detection

LifeCraft AI automatically:

* Extracts skills from resumes
* Categorizes skills into domains
* Detects missing or weak skills

This helps users identify areas that need improvement.

### 📚 Personalized Learning Recommendations

Based on identified skill gaps, the platform suggests:

* Relevant learning resources
* Recommended technologies
* Structured learning paths

This enables focused and efficient learning.

### 🎤 AI-Based Interview Preparation

Generate:

* Domain-specific interview questions
* Key concepts for revision
* Topic-wise preparation guidance

Helping users prepare confidently for interviews.

### 🛣️ Career Roadmap

A guided roadmap that takes users through:

Learning → Projects → Experience → Interview Preparation → Job Readiness
